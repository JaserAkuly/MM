What We Can Do For You

Nikah

In Islam, a Nikah (marriage ceremony) is a covenant with Allah to honor, respect, care for and love your spouse; it is a legally binding contract. Download our Marriage Certificate.

Religious Counseling

Its not easy to cope with challenging issues that rise each and everyday in our lives. Many times, we need someone to advise us in our lives. There are always solutions to our problems. The best advice comes from Islamic values and principles.

Funeral Services

Death is a very painful and emotional time, yet one that may be filled with hope and mercy. Makkah Masjid partners with Rahma Funeral Home to provide funeral services.

Islamic School

Learn from the Past. Live the Present. Plan for the Future. Classes and lessons for all ages. Bring your friends.

Youth

The youth program is a strong tool for the Muslim youth to use in order to better the character and strengthen their bond amongst themselves.

Legal and Family Advising

We provide legal and family advising. Please contact us for more information.

About Us

Shortly About us

Makkah Masjid was founded in 1993 as the Islamic Directions Enhancement Association (IDEA) to provide Islamic educational, social and cultural services to the Muslim community in the region and to promote the teachings of Islam to inquiring Muslims and non-Muslims. In Ramadan of 2007, to better serve the religious needs of our community and prepare our children in an Islamic environment, IDEA purchased the Makkah Masjid property. This property consists of 2.66 acres. The building on the property is over 10,000 square feet, and the parking lot has a capacity of over 200. This location has given us the ability to accommodate for large and small events and gatherings, classes, and includes special rooms for sisters and children.

Garland Makkah Masjid is a non-profit Islamic organization located in Garland, TX. It is a Mosque and Community Center serving the Muslims of Garland, Richardson and entire DFW area.All activities and services conducted by Makkah Masjid are according to the teachings of Islam, as determined by the Quran, and the Sunnah (tradition) of Prophet Mohammed (Peace be upon him) and the pious ones (Sahaba & Awliyah).
